Hot Dogs

 

Are they considered sandwiches?

'A Sandwich, 2 or more seperate pieces of material(bread, lettuce, etc) that has something in between it. so, an oreo is technically a sandwich. The act of sandwiching something is to press it in between two or more objects right? Now a hotdog, it is surrounded by a solid piece of bread or what have you. So, it's not a sandwich.. Now, if that bun splits at the splits at the seam then it is now two pieces of bread and thus turning the hotdog into a sandwich. In conclusion A hotdog is not a sandwich because to be a sandwich it has to be between two or more individual pieces of something such as bread, lettuce, etc...'
- Warren Buffet
